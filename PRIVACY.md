# Privacy Policy for Resurface

**Last Updated: January 18, 2026**

## Overview

Resurface is a Chrome extension that helps you save and organize web pages. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
When you save a page, Resurface stores the following information **locally on your device**:
- Page URL
- Page title
- Page description (meta description)
- Search query context (if you came from a search engine)
- Topics and categories you assign
- Timestamp of when you saved the page

### What We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identification information
- Browsing history beyond what you explicitly save
- Passwords or form data
- Financial information
- Location data

## Data Storage

**All data is stored locally** on your device using Chrome's IndexedDB storage. Your saved pages never leave your browser.

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `storage` | Store your saved pages locally |
| `tabs` | Access tab URL/title when you save |
| `activeTab` | Extract page content when you save |
| `scripting` | Inject save UI and overlays |
| `history` | Show history in New Tab search |
